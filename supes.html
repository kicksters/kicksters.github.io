Superman, also known as Kal-el on his home planet of Krypton and as Clark Kent on Earth. Superman, under the yellow sun has many powers and has been shown to expands his vast list of power after becoming Golden Superman. The most commonly known powers he has are superhuman strength, speed, senses, endurance, longevity, flight, x-ray and heat vision, flight, and invulnerability to non-magical attacks. The shield on his chest has a symbol of an "S" which stands for hope on his home palnet and he has brought that hope to Earth. All of his powers have been shown to have no bounds, he has been shown moving planets and whole solar systems on his back, also he lifted up the being known as, Spectre, who weighs an eternity!
