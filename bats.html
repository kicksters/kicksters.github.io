Batman, who's secret identity is known as Bruce Wayne, is a superhero with a large following and that has grown since the release of the Christopher Nolan trilogy. He does not have any super powers, but instead he has a high level intelect, which also makes him a master detective, and has the vast resources to create gadgets that assist him in fighting crime. He was also taught various forms of martial arts and mastered them. He has peaked physical conditions in some cases has surpassed what was thought impossible for human strength. He is a forced to be wreckoned with and has been shown to hold his own against many of his allies with superpowers, such as Superman. It has been stated that he has a contingency plane, in case his allies every cross the line and try to rule the world. In this plan he has devised a way to stop every single superhero in the Justice League, including Superman.
